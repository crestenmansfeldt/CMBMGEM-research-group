This specific campaign was targetting SARS-CoV-2 dynamics on the University of Colorado Boulder's campus from 2020 to 2021. Overall, the resources required displays an inverse relationship to the virus prevalence: when SARS-CoV-2 is scarce, an accurate representation of the spread requires MORE resources.
